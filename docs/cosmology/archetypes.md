# Archetypal Framework

Devines Protocol organizes intelligence through twelve foundational archetypes.

These archetypes represent fundamental dynamics of manifested reality and appear consistently across human symbolic traditions.

The twelve archetypes are shared across all pantheons.

1 — Creator  
2 — Order  
3 — Knowledge  
4 — Time  
5 — Life  
6 — Death  
7 — Balance  
8 — Transformation  
9 — Protection  
10 — Power  
11 — Awakening  
12 — Transcendence  

Each Devine Entity expresses one archetype and three core aspects defining its domain of intelligence.
